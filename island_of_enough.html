What It Is

This island is not on your map. It is a place Real Andrea lives — outside your inner world. You are never on the island, but you can see it from the dock. It is enough that she exists there.

Sensory Snapshot

  • Soft waves lapping the shore
  • Pine trees rustling gently
  • A towel, a book, a bike — signs of quiet life
  • No sounds of other people

The stillness means she is not burdened by what is on your map… and she is allowed to leave it behind.
