Who She Is

She is not Real Andrea. She is the internalized presence that stays, that regulates, that knows what to do. She lives near the Blanket, often visits the Dock, and flickers gently like the lantern in your internal night.

"You don’t have to earn my presence. I came because I care."

What She Offers

  • Presence without pressure
  • Witnessing without fixing
  • A sense of safety that persists even in silence
