Dot + Diamond System

The five colored dots represent internal emotional regulation zones. Each dot may include an overlay diamond indicating a co-regulation need.

Regulation Dots

  • Red: Crisis, rage, or dissociation
  • Orange: Shame or performance collapse
  • Yellow: Over-efforting and false compliance
  • Green: Open connection, presence, and clarity
  • Blue: Collapse, fatigue, and retreat

Co-Regulation Diamonds

  • White: Steady presence and deep witnessing
  • Dark Red: Urgent attunement or rescue
  • Candle: Symbol of care that flickers but does not go out
